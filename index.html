effortless music play

Home entertainment today is all about a compelling experience, easy access to virtually unlimited content, and effortless use. Our smart home app can serve as the foundation for the different ways you enjoy audio.

how it works

Using artificial intelligence and facial recognition our mobile app uses a smart device that recoginzes your face as you enter a room. Our advanced algorithm accesses your most liked tracks from Spotify and automatically plays them for you to enjoy.

getting started

With a one time set up process, you don't have to fuss with signing in every time you want to hear your favorite tunes. Simply create your profile by snaping a selfie and log into your spotify account.

Set up your device in any room of your home. Our design is small enough to mount on a wall, shelf, or picture frame.

intelligent features

Personalization

Add multiple user profiles for everyone in your household to enjoy. Set up a device in every room to take the music flow with you as you go about your day.

Endless Customization

For future enhancements, users will be able to customize their experience by adding playlist and personalized stations before going home.
